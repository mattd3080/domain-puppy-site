FAQ

Why did you make Domain Puppy?
When I'm vibe coding, I absolutely hate leaving the terminal to deal with a GUI. I've found that many domain services are slow. And the bulk search features are disappointing, after experiencing the magic of AI. Domain Puppy solves that by letting you stay in terminal for all of your domain brainstorming and research. We use a series of APIs to see if your ideas are available. And then when you're ready to acquire your domain, we'll open up a browser tab for you.
